Story of Coffee and Bread

It would a such luxurious experience if you could have bread with coffee.

Cappucino? Latte? Mocha? Flat White? Macchiato? Dirty?
None of these concoctions hold a candle to my ultimate go-to, regardless of the season – Cold Brew. Why?
Because I am a certified, unbashed aficionado of this chilled drink.
There's something utterly captivating about its simplicity yet hidden depths of flavor. Cold Brew isn't just a drink; it's an experience, a journey through nuanced tastes and cool vibes. So while others debate over frothy or foamy, I'll be sipping on the pure essence of refreshment. J oin me in the adventure of Cold Brew – where every sip is a revelation!
BUT, what if I could have it with some bread?
